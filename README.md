# 🌦️ Weather Dashboard

Welcome to my **Weather Dashboard** — a modern, minimal, and super-cool web app that lets you check the weather for any city on Earth, instantly! Built with love, glassmorphism, and a sprinkle of JavaScript magic.

---

## 🚀 What Makes This Dashboard Awesome?

- **Live Weather Search:** Type any city, hit search, and get real-time weather, temperature, wind, humidity, pressure, sunrise/sunset, visibility, and even Air Quality Index (AQI) — all in a beautiful card.
- **Search via Map:** Don't want to type? Click the 📍 'Find on Map' button, tap anywhere on the interactive map, and boom — weather for that spot, no typing needed!
- **Dark Mode:** Your eyes, your vibe. Toggle between light and dark themes with a single click. Your choice is remembered.
- **Live Clock:** Always know the current day, date, and time — updates every second.
- **Search History:** Your last 10 searches are saved as clickable buttons. Jump back to any city in a flash (and clear history anytime).
- **Global Cities at a Glance:** See weather for major cities around the world in a responsive grid. Click any card to zoom in on that city's weather.
- **Smooth Animations & Glassmorphism:** Every interaction feels fresh, thanks to subtle animations and a glassy, modern UI powered by Tailwind CSS.

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

- **Frontend Only:** No backend, no build tools — just open `index.html` in your browser and you're live!
- **Powered by APIs:** Weather and AQI data come from OpenWeatherMap. The map uses Leaflet.js and OpenStreetMap tiles. City names from map clicks are found using Nominatim's free reverse geocoding.
- **No Tracking, No Ads:** 100% privacy-friendly. All your data stays in your browser.

---

## ✨ Try It Out

1. Clone or download this repo.
2. Open `index.html` in your favorite browser.
3. Enter a city or click 'Find on Map'.
4. Enjoy the weather, anywhere, anytime!
